About MoneyPit

Built for builders.
Not posers.

Every project car has a story. The late nights, the blown budgets, the "just one more part." MoneyPit was built for the people who live that story — not to show off a finished product, but to document the whole damn journey.

Log every mod. Track every dollar. Share the wins and the setbacks. This isn't Instagram for cars — it's a build diary with a community attached.

For the casual enthusiast
Weekend warrior? Daily driver gone wrong? Doesn't matter. MoneyPit gives you a place to document whatever you're building and connect with people doing the same.
For the serious builder
Track every dollar, every part, every milestone. Build your reputation in a community that respects the grind. Share your affiliate links. Own your build log.
No algorithm. No noise.
A feed curated by who you follow — not what some platform thinks you want to buy. Just builds, parts, and people who actually know what they're talking about.
